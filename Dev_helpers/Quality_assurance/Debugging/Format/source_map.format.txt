VLQ_INT:
  - represents a signed integer value within range of 32 bits integers
  - encoding:
     - VLQ (see integers doc)
     - sequence of 6-bits bytes, translated into base64 A-Za-z+/
     - first bit of each byte is 1 if there is a next byte
     - "zigzag encoding": last byte's last bit is signedness
     - i.e. each 6-bits byte:
        - last char (MSB):
            0 to 15:   A C E G I K M O Q S U W Y a c e
            -0 to -15: B D F H J L N P R T V X Z b d f
        - not last char:
            0 to 15:   g i k m o q s u w y 0 2 4 6 8 +
            -0 to -15: h j l n p r t v x z 1 3 5 7 9 /
